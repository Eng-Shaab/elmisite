Training Program

Elmi Academy had designed a set of core training in IT and computer science for its trainees. These courses are presumed to render the trainee sufficiently well- versed with
the essential principles of the subject upon complete mastery of the course’s material. The training courses are specially selected for the trainees, as they can provide him/her a good
insight and understanding of the subject and further make him/her able to use the skill in practical situations and day to day operations in the working environment:

01

Application developer and Database Administrators Training

• Front Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) • Front End Framework (Angular, VeuJs, React Bootstrap) • Back-End Development Languages (NodeJS, Python, C#) • Back-End Frameworks (ExpressJS, Flutter, Ruby Rails) • Database Administration (SQL, SQL Server, MangoDB, MySQL)

02

Professional Network Engineering & Cloud Computing Trainings

• Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) • Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) • Microsoft Certified System Administrator (MCSA) • Microsoft Azure Certifications

03

Cybersecurity Training

• CompTIA Security+ • Fortinet Network Security Professional • Cisco Cyber Ops Certification • eLearn Security Penetration Testing (eJPT)

04

IT Service Management Training

• ITIL Service Management • COBIT framework for IT governance • DevOps • Foundation of IT project Management • Agile Project Management • Business Analysis and Process Management

05

Multimedia and Digital Marketing

• Digital Marketing • Graphic Design • Motion Graphic
